== 行列式的转置 transposed determinant

所谓"转置", 就是把"行"变"列".

\begin{align}
D=\left| \begin{matrix}
	1&		2&		3\\
	1&		1&		1\\
	8&		8&		8\\
\end{matrix} \right|,\ \overset{\text{转置}}{\rightarrow}\ D^T=\underset{\text{行变列了}}{\underbrace{\left| \begin{matrix}
	1&		1&		8\\
	2&		1&		8\\
	3&		1&		8\\
\end{matrix} \right|}}
\end{align}


---


== 性质

==== stem:[ D^T = D]

即: 转置后的行列式的值, 与转置前相同. 值不变.
