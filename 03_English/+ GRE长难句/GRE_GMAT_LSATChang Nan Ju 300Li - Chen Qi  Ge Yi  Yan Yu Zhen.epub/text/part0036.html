Most striking among the many asymmetries evident in an adult flatfish is eye placement: before maturity one eye migrates, so that in an adult flatfish both eyes are on the same side of the head.

标识

Most striking among the many asymmetries 1[evident in an adult flatfish] is 2[eye placement]: before maturity one eye migrates, 3{so that in an adult flatfish both eyes are on the same side 4[of the head]}.

难点

1. 形容词加介词结构倒装。evident in an adult flatfish修饰asymmetries。

2. 主语倒装。句子正常语序为Eye placement is most striking among the many asymmetries evident in an adult flatfish。

3. 长句。so that引导的结果状语从句。

译文

成年比目鱼身上存在着许多不对称的地方，其中眼睛位置的不对称最引人注目：在比目鱼成年之前，其中一只眼睛会发生移动，因此成年比目鱼的两只眼睛都在头的同一侧。
