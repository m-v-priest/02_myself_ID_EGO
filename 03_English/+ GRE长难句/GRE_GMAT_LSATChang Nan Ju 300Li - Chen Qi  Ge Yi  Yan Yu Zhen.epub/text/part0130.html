We have no pride in our growing interdependence, in the fact that our system can serve others, that we are able to help those in need; empty boasts from the past make us ashamed of our present achievements, make us try to forget or deny them, move away from them.

标识

We have no pride 1[in our growing interdependence], 2we have no pride in the fact 3[that our system can serve others], 4we have no pride in the fact 5[that we are able to help those 6[in need]]; empty boasts 7[from the past] make us ashamed of our present achievements, make us try to forget or deny them, move away from them.

难点

1. 介词结构倒装。in our growing interdependence修饰pride。

2. 省略。平行结构中省略了we have no pride。

3. 同位语从句。that引导的同位语从句进一步解释fact。

4. 省略。平行结构中省略了we have no pride in the fact。

5. 同位语从句。that引导的同位语从句进一步解释fact。

6. 介词结构倒装。in need修饰those。

7. 介词结构倒装。from the past修饰empty boasts。

译文

我们没有为我们相互依赖的程度越来越高而感到骄傲；我们没有为我们的制度可以服务于他人这个事实而感到骄傲；我们没有为我们有能力帮助那些需要帮助的人这个事实而感到骄傲；对过去毫无意义的吹嘘使得我们耻于现在的成就，使得我们尝试忘记或者否认它们，摆脱它们。
