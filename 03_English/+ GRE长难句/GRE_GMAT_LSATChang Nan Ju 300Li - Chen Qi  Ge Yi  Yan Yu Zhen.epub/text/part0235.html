The term deconstruction implies that the text has been put together like a building or a piece of machinery, and that it is in need of being taken apart, not so much in order to repair it as to demonstrate underlying inadequacies, false assumptions, and inherent contradictions.

译文

“解构”这个术语暗示了文本就像是一座建筑或者一台机器一样，是被拼装起来的。这个术语还暗示了文本需要被拆开，与其说是为了还原文本的本来面目，不如说是为了展示文本中潜藏的不足、错误的假设和内在的矛盾。
