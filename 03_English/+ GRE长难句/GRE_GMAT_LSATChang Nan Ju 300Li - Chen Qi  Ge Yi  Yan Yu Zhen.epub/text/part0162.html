Such philosophical concerns as the mind-body problem or, more generally, the nature of human knowledge they believe, are basic human questions whose tentative philosophical solutions have served as the necessary foundations on which all other intellectual speculation has rested.

标识

Such philosophical concerns 1[as the mind-body problem] 2{or, more generally, 3as the nature 4[of human knowledge] 5[that they believe]}, are basic human questions 6[whose tentative philosophical solutions have served as the necessary foundations] 7[on which all other intellectual speculation has rested].

难点

1. 介词结构倒装。as the mind-body problem修饰philosophical concerns。

2. 长句。or连接的平行结构。

3. 平行结构省略。or连接的平行结构中省略了as。

4. 介词结构倒装。of human knowledge修饰nature。

5. 定语从句。they believe是修饰philosophical concerns的定语从句，此处省略引导词that。

6. 定语从句。whose引导的定语从句修饰questions。

7. 定语从句。on which引导的定语从句修饰foundations。

译文

他们认为哲学所关注的问题都是基本的人类问题，比如心智与肉身的关系问题，或者更为普遍的诸如人类知识的本质的问题。对于这些哲学问题的初步回答已经可以作为探讨其他所有知识的必要基础。
