In order to understand the nature of the ecologist's investigation, we may think of the densitydependent effects on growth parameters as the “signal” ecologists are trying to isolate and interpret, one that tends to make the population increase from relatively low values or decrease from relatively high ones, while the density-independent effects act to produce “noise” in the population dynamics.

译文

为了理解生态学家研究的实质，我们也许应该将关于增长参数的种群密度无关的效应视为一个“信号”，一些生态学家正在尝试分离这个效应，并解释其中的原因。这个“信号”一旦出现，就说明种群数量将会维持在一个相对稳定的水平，尽管与种群密度无关的效应将会使种群数量出现“异常值”。
