标识

1[It] can be inferred from the passage that the author would be most likely to agree with which of the following statements 2[regarding socioeconomic class] 3{and 4which of the following statements regarding support 5[for the rebel and Loyalist causes] 6[during the American Revolutionary War]}?

难点

1. 主语倒装。it是形式主语，指代真正的主语that the author would...during the American Revolutionary War。

2. 分词结构倒装。regarding socioeconomic class修饰statements。

3. 长句。and连接的平行结构。

4. 平行结构省略。and连接的平行结构中省略了which of the following statements regarding。

5. 介词结构倒装。for the rebel and Loyalist causes修饰support。

6. 介词结构倒装。during the American Revolutionary War修饰socioeconomic class and support。

译文
