The consumption of protein increases blood concentration of the other amino acids much more, proportionately, than it does that of tryptophan.

标识

The consumption 1[of protein] increases blood concentration 2[of the other amino acids] much more, proportionately, than it does that 3[of tryptophan].

难点

1. 介词结构倒装。of protein修饰consumption。

2. 介词结构倒装。of the other amino acids修饰blood concentration。

3. 介词结构倒装。of tryptophan修饰that。

译文

蛋白质的消耗大大增加了其他氨基酸在血液中的浓度，从比例上，远远高于蛋白质的消耗增加的色氨酸在血液中的浓度的程度。
