By the middle of eighteenth century, all of these colonies except four were headed by Royal Governors appointed by the King and perceived as bearing a relation to the people of the colony similar to that of the King to the English people.

标识

1{By the middle of eighteenth century}, all of these colonies 2[except four] were headed 3[by Royal Governors] 4[appointed by the King] 5{and 6[perceived as bearing a relation] 7[to the people] 8[of the colony] 9[similar to that] 10[of the King] 11[to the English people]}.

难点

1. 长句。by引导的时间状语。

2. 介词结构倒装。except four修饰all of these colonies。

3. 介词结构倒装。by Royal Governors修饰headed。

4. 分词结构倒装。appointed by the King修饰Royal Governors。

5. 长句。and连接的平行结构。

6. 分词结构倒装。perceived as bearing a relation修饰Royal Governors。

7. 介词结构倒装。to the people修饰relation。

8. 介词结构倒装。of the colony修饰people。

9. 形容词加介词结构倒装。similar to that修饰relation。

10. 介词结构倒装。of the King修饰that，that指代a relation。

11. 介词结构倒装。to the English people修饰that。

译文

到18世纪中期，除了四个殖民地之外的所有这些殖民地都由英国国王任命的皇室总督来领导，这些皇室总督与殖民地人民的关系就好比英国国王与英国人民的关系。
