That each large firm will act with consideration of its own needs and thus avoid selling its products for more than its competitors' charge is commonly recognized by advocates of freemarket economic theories.

译文

每家大公司都会考虑自身的需求而行动，并由此避免比竞争对手把商品卖得更贵，这一点得到了自由市场的经济理论支持者的广泛认可。
