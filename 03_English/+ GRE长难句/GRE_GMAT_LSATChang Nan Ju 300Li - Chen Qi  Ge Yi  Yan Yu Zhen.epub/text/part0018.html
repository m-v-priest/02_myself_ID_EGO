标识

1[More remarkable than the origin has been the persistence] 2[of such sex segregation] 3[in twentieth-century industry].

难点

3. 介词结构倒装。in twentieth-century industry修饰sex segregation。

译文

在20世纪的工业中，这种性别歧视的持续比其起源更引人注目。
