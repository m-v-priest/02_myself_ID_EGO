=== 对数公式

==== stem:[ ln ab = ln a + ln b]

---

==== stem:[ ln \frac{a} {b} = ln a - ln b]

---

==== stem:[ log_a b = \frac{log_c b} {log_c a}]

---

==== stem:[ ln a^b = b ln a]

---

==== stem:[ ln \[f(m) - f(h)\] = ln f(m-h)  = ln f - ln(m-h)]
